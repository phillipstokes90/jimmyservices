FAQ

HVAC Questions Philadelphia Customers Ask

Do you offer same-day HVAC repair in Philadelphia?

Yes. We prioritize same-day service for HVAC emergencies across Philadelphia, Bucks County, Delaware County, Montgomery County, and surrounding areas. Call (803) 221-7013 and we'll tell you our earliest available time.

What HVAC brands do you service?

All of them — Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, York, Goodman, American Standard, Bryant, and more. If it blows air, we service it.

Is a heat pump worth it in Philadelphia?

Yes — especially now. Heat pumps qualify for up to $2,000 in federal tax credits under the Inflation Reduction Act. Philadelphia's climate is well-suited for heat pumps. They heat and cool in one system, and they're 2–3x more efficient than gas furnaces. We'll walk you through the math before you decide.

How long does an HVAC repair take?

Most standard repairs — capacitors, contactors, refrigerant, thermostat issues — are completed in 1–3 hours on the same visit. Compressor replacements or full system installs are typically scheduled separately and take a full day.

What if my system is too old to repair?

We'll tell you honestly. If a repair costs more than 50% of a new system's price and your unit is over 10 years old, we'll say so. We'd rather lose a repair job and earn a customer for life than oversell you a band-aid fix.
