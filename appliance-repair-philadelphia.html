FAQ

Appliance Repair Questions Philadelphia Customers Ask

Is it worth repairing a refrigerator or should I replace it?

The general rule: if the repair costs less than 50% of a replacement and the appliance is under 10–12 years old, repair it. David will give you an honest assessment. He loses no money either way — he makes the same margin on a repair as on the referral for a new one, so there's no pressure.

What appliance brands do you service?

All major brands: Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Frigidaire, Bosch, KitchenAid, Maytag, Kenmore, Electrolux, Viking, and more. If it plugs in or runs on gas and lives in your home or business, we service it.

Do you carry parts on the van?

We stock the most common parts for common failures — motors, heating elements, pump assemblies, belts, and capacitors. Many repairs are done same visit. If we need to order a part, we schedule a return visit as soon as it arrives, typically 1–2 days.

Is the $59 diagnostic fee applied to the repair?

Yes. If you decide to proceed with the repair, the $59 diagnostic fee is credited toward your repair total. You only pay the diagnostic if you choose not to repair.

Do you repair commercial refrigeration?

Yes — walk-in coolers, display cases, commercial ice machines, and prep table units. We serve restaurants, delis, and small businesses across Philadelphia and Delaware County.
