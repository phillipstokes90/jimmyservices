FAQ

Appliance Repair Questions

Is it worth repairing or should I replace?

If the repair costs less than 50% of a replacement and the appliance is under 10–12 years old, repair it. We give you an honest assessment — we make the same margin either way, so there's no pressure.

What appliance brands do you service?

All major brands: Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Frigidaire, Bosch, KitchenAid, Maytag, Kenmore, Electrolux, Viking, and more.

Do you carry parts on the van?

We stock the most common parts — motors, heating elements, pump assemblies, belts, and capacitors. Many repairs are done same visit. If we need to order a part, we return within 1–2 days.

Is the $59 diagnostic applied to the repair?

Yes. If you decide to proceed, the $59 is credited toward your repair total. You only pay it if you choose not to repair.

Do you repair commercial appliances?

Yes — walk-in coolers, display cases, commercial ice machines, and prep units. We serve restaurants and small businesses across Philadelphia and Delaware County.
