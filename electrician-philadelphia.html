Electrical Problems Philadelphia Homeowners Face

Older rowhomes and aging panels cause most of these. Don't guess with electrical work — call us.

Breaker Keeps Tripping

Overloaded circuit or a failing breaker. We diagnose the cause instead of just resetting it and hoping.

Flickering or Dimming Lights

Often a loose connection or overloaded circuit — sometimes a sign of a bigger panel issue. Worth checking before it's a fire risk.

Old Fuse Box or Undersized Panel

100-amp panels and old fuse boxes can't handle modern homes. We upgrade to 200-amp service safely and to code.

Not Enough Outlets

Extension cords running everywhere is a hazard, not a fix. We add outlets and dedicated circuits where you actually need them.

Knob-and-Tube or Aluminum Wiring

Common in older Philadelphia homes. Insurers and inspectors flag it — we assess and rewire safely, room by room if needed.

FAQ

Electrical Questions Philadelphia Homeowners Ask

Do I need a panel upgrade?

If you have a 60 or 100-amp panel, still use fuses, or your breakers trip regularly, you likely need an upgrade. We assess your panel on-site and give you an honest answer along with a flat quote.

Do you handle knob-and-tube or aluminum wiring?

Yes — very common in Philadelphia's older housing stock. We assess the scope and can often rewire room-by-room to manage cost.

Are you licensed for commercial electrical work?

Yes, licensed for both residential and commercial electrical work throughout Philadelphia County and surrounding areas.

Can you install an EV charger too?

Yes — EV charger wiring is one of our most popular jobs. See our EV charger installation page for flat-rate pricing.

Do you pull permits for electrical work?

Yes, for any work that requires it — panel upgrades, rewiring, new circuits — we handle the permit and inspection process.
