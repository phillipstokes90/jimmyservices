FAQ

Common Questions

Do you serve both residential and commercial customers?

Yes — we work with homeowners, landlords, small businesses, restaurants, retail stores, and offices throughout the Philadelphia, PA area. Same quality, same team, same number to call.

How fast can you come out?

For emergencies, we do our best to respond same day — often within a few hours. For scheduled jobs, we typically get you in within 1–3 business days. Call (803) 221-7013 and we'll tell you exactly what's available.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Jimmy Services is fully licensed and insured in the state of Pennsylvania. We carry liability insurance and are compliant with all state and local codes for every trade we perform.

Do you give free estimates?

We offer diagnostic visits starting at $89, which covers the trip and evaluation. If you move forward with the repair, that fee is applied toward your total. Major projects get a written estimate before any work begins.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Philadelphia, Bucks County, Delaware County, Montgomery County, Chester County, Cherry Hill NJ, and surrounding areas. If you're not sure, just call and we'll let you know.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes — we have residential Home Care Plans starting at $49/month and commercial Business Care Plans starting at $129/month. Both include priority scheduling, annual tune-ups, and discounted repair labor. See our Plans & Pricing section above.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and Zelle. Payment is due upon completion of the job, or a deposit may be required for larger projects.

Do you have reviews I can read?

Yes — search "Jimmy Services Philadelphia" on Google to read verified customer reviews. We're proud of our 5-star rating built entirely on word of mouth across Philadelphia, Bucks County, Delaware County, Montgomery County, and surrounding areas.

Can you handle both my home and my business?

Absolutely. Many of our Philadelphia-area customers use us for both their home and their business. One number, one team, one relationship — whether it's a residential AC replacement in Doylestown or a commercial refrigeration emergency in Center City.
