FAQ

Common Questions

Do you serve both residential and commercial customers?

Yes — we work with homeowners, landlords, small businesses, restaurants, retail stores, and offices throughout the Philadelphia area. Same quality, same team, same number to call.

How fast can you come out?

For emergencies, we respond same day — often within a few hours. Scheduled jobs typically within 1–3 business days. Call (803) 221-7013 and we'll tell you what's available.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Jimmy Services is fully licensed and insured in the state of Pennsylvania. We carry liability insurance and are compliant with all state and local codes for every trade we perform.

Do you give free estimates?

Diagnostic visits start at $89, which covers the trip and evaluation. If you move forward with the repair, that fee is applied toward your total. Major projects get a written estimate before any work begins.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Philadelphia, Bucks County, Delaware County, Montgomery County, Chester County, Cherry Hill NJ, Wilmington DE, and surrounding areas. If you're not sure, just call and we'll let you know.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and Zelle. Payment is due upon completion of the job. A deposit may be required for larger projects.

Do you use subcontractors?

No. The person who answers the phone is the person who shows up. We don't hand jobs off to subcontractors. Every job is done by our own team.
